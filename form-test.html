How to Verify Email Delivery

  1. Check your inbox at martin@lumenosis.com for the test email
  2. If using FormSubmit.co for the first time, you may need to confirm your email address
  3. Check your spam folder if you don't see the email
  4. When live submissions come in, you'll see them at the same email address

Troubleshooting

  • Make sure your domain's DNS is properly configured
  • Verify that FormSubmit.co supports your email domain
  • If emails go to spam, add FormSubmit to your safe senders list
